Who We Are

A Family Recipe.
A Neighborhood Institution.

What started as a backyard cookout tradition has turned into Riverside's favorite gathering spot. Jack Stack and his two sons, Milo and Eli, opened Stack & Sons in 2009 with one cast-iron griddle, a family recipe passed down from Jack's father, and a whole lot of heart.

Every smash burger we serve is pressed hard and fast on a screaming-hot flat-top — just the way Grandpa Stack taught us. No shortcuts, no freezers, no apologies. Just fresh beef, real ingredients, and a kitchen run by people who love what they cook.
